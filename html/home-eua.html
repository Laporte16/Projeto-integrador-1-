About Us

Está-no-ar is an informative website with the aim of teaching what air pollution is and how it happens, showing how much it can be harmful to humanity and the world, and how air pollution can be contributing to that other pollutions occur. The site will have six pages in total (home-causes-urban pollution-what affects us-pollution dominoes-contact), the home page will present the site and the topic it addresses, while the causes page will address the causes of air pollution problems. What is air pollution


Air or atmospheric pollution can be defined as the result of the release of large amounts of solid or liquid particles into the atmosphere with the potential to cause environmental impacts or harm human health. Among these substances we can mention industrial dust, aerosols, black smoke, solvents, acids and hydrocarbons. When we burn something like wood, we are keeping the same amount of carbon inside the system, but sending it into the atmosphere in the form of carbon dioxide. However, when we burn a fossil fuel such as mineral coal or oil, we are bringing into the same system enormous amounts of substances that, long ago, were fossilized.
