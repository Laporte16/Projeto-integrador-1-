Domino pollution


Domino effect and the theory is attributed John Foster Dulles.
The expression Domino Theory, which uses the image of one piece knocking down the next, that is to say an action that triggers others just as the atmospheric influence that reaches the air affects the quality of the air of living beings causing diseases and consequently forests, soil, rivers and lakes

Greenhouse effect

The greenhouse effectis the increase in the temperature of the Earth by the retention of heat by certain atmospheric gases, and combustion intensifies it and causes global warming.

Among the main consequences of the greenhouse effect are:
  • Melting ice capsand rising sea levels.
  • Flooding in northern latitudes and the Equatorial Pacific.
  • Extinction of species and irreparable damage to diverse ecosystems.
  • Water scarcity in some regions.
  • Health problems caused by increased heat.
